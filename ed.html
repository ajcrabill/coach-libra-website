How your book
gets written.

Five unhurried steps that fit around the school year. Most of the heavy lifting happens off your plate — you mostly just talk (record a voice memo on your phone and share it with me), and confirm.

01

We learn your voice

Share a few things you've already written — a newsletter to families, a board memo, a graduation speech. Coach Libra builds a true print of how you sound.

02

We organize your outline

A few good questions — answer them however you like, even by voice memo — and your hard-won lessons become a clear outline, benchmarked against the best books in education and leadership.

03

We construct your draft

Coach Libra asks the questions; you answer by voice or email between meetings. We build the manuscript from your answers, chapter by chapter, in your voice.

04

You refine your draft

You read, react, and steer. Small nudges, not heavy lifting. The book bends toward exactly what you meant.

05

A polished manuscript

Rigorously edited and held to the standard of the highest-rated books in your category — then delivered, ready for the world.
