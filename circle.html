The Author's Circle

A circle of authors,
all on the
same journey.

Every month, the authors writing with Coach Libra come together — a community of people doing exactly what you're doing, cheering each other on toward the same goal: taking what they know and carrying it to a far wider audience.

Begin your book → Authors supporting authors, every month.

You're surrounded by people who get it.

Writing a book is a strange, solitary thing — until you're in a room of people doing it too. Once a month, the authors working with Coach Libra gather to compare notes, trade encouragement, and remind each other why it matters: every one of you is working to take something hard-won and put it in front of a far bigger audience. Bring a stuck chapter, a small win, a question, or just come to listen. You'll leave knowing you're not doing this alone.

Why it matters

A whole community on your side

Ⅰ

Authors who get it

Everyone in the room is on the same road: turning hard-won expertise into a book worth reading. The encouragement, the war stories, and the quiet "oh — me too" come from people who actually understand the work.

Ⅱ

Candid and confidential

What's said in the Circle stays in the Circle. The gatherings aren't recorded, so you can speak openly — about the doubts as much as the breakthroughs.

Ⅲ

Momentum toward your readers

A standing monthly rhythm keeps your book moving and keeps the goal in view: not just finishing, but getting your message out to the audience that needs to hear it.

How it works

Nothing to sign up for.

Once your book is underway, the invitation comes to you by email each month — the date, the time, and a private link. Come to the gatherings that fit your life and skip the ones that don't; nothing is required, and you're always welcome back.

Begin your book

Write it among people like you.

Start your manuscript with Coach Libra and you'll be among a community of authors all working toward the same thing: getting their message into the world. Leave your name and we'll reach out when a place opens.
