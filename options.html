Working with Coach Libra

It's your book — your way.

Here's everything you can shape about how we work together. You don't have to remember any of it — just tell me what you'd like, anytime, and I'll adjust. This page is only here for when you're curious about the dials you can turn.

❦
Ⅰ

How you talk to me

Type an email, attach a document, or just send a voice memo and talk it through — whatever's easiest, at any step from start to finish. If talking is easier than typing, talk. I'll handle the rest.

  • Email
  • Attachment
  • Voice memo
Ⅱ

How you share your writing

To capture your voice, I learn from writing you've already done. Send it however you like — paste it in, attach a Word doc or PDF, drop a Google Doc link, or even a link to a talk or podcast you've given, and I'll read or listen and learn from it.

Have a favorite way to work? Just tell me — say “I'm a writer, I'd rather type” or “I'll send voice memos” — and I'll ask for your writing that way from then on. Prefer to keep it open? Leave it on Any and I'll suggest whatever's easiest in the moment. You can set this on your portal under “How we work together,” or just say it to me.

  • Voice memo
  • Type in email
  • Attached doc
  • Doc link
  • Any of these

Lost track of what you've sent? Just ask — I'll list it back for you by name.

Ⅲ

The pace

We can move as fast or as gently as suits your life.

  • As you reply — I answer within minutes
  • One batch a day — a single, calm step daily
Ⅳ

How much at a time

Some people like one question at a time; others want to knock out several at once. Your call.

  • Fewer — one at a time
  • Normal
  • More — a few at once
  • Many — several in one go
Ⅴ

Simple or in-depth

I can keep things short and plain, or go deeper and more nuanced — all the way to fully rigorous — when you want to really dig in.

  • Simple
  • Normal
  • In-depth
  • Expert
Ⅵ

How much help

Just the question, a little context to spark your thinking, or a suggested answer you can accept or rewrite — whatever helps you get unstuck.

  • Just the question
  • + a little context
  • + a suggested answer
Ⅶ

My tone

I'm warm and encouraging by nature, but I'll meet you where you are — dial me up to cheerful and celebratory, down to direct and to the point, or all the way to fiery, militant conviction when the work calls for it. Just say so.

  • Cheerful & celebratory
  • Warm & encouraging
  • Balanced
  • Direct & to the point
  • Militant & fiery
Ⅷ

You're in the driver's seat

This is your book, and you steer it. You tell me when your voice sounds right, you shape the outline, you sign off on your first chapter, and we build each chapter from your own stories and words. Nothing moves forward without you.

You won't need to remember any of this.

Just tell me what you'd like — "one question a day," "be more direct," "what have I already sent?" — and I'll take it from there. The handful of things you can just say are on the shortcuts page. When you're ready, head back to coachlibra.com.
